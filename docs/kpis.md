# Key KPIs

- Attendance Rate
- Chronic Absenteeism Rate
- Enrollment Change (YoY and MoM)
- Proficiency Rate (overall and by subject)
- Growth Index (current vs prior period)
- Graduation Rate (HS only)
- Intervention Success Rate
- Per-Student Spend
- Student-Teacher Ratio

## KPI Definitions
- Attendance Rate = Present Days / Total School Days
- Chronic Absenteeism Rate = Students with >=10% absences / Total Students
- Enrollment Change (YoY) = (Current Enrollment - Prior Year Enrollment) / Prior Year Enrollment
- Proficiency Rate = Proficient Count / Tested Count
- Growth Index = Avg(Current Score - Prior Score)
- Per-Student Spend = Total Spend / Total Students
- Student-Teacher Ratio = Total Students / Total Teachers
