# Business Case

District leaders need a single, reliable view of student outcomes, attendance risk, enrollment shifts, program effectiveness, and resource allocation. Today, these metrics often live in separate systems, which slows decision-making and makes it difficult to identify early-warning signals.

This dashboard centralizes the data and answers critical questions:
- Which schools are improving or declining in proficiency?
- Where is chronic absenteeism rising, and among which subgroups?
- Which programs correlate with better outcomes?
- Are resources aligned with student need and impact?

The goal is to enable faster interventions, reduce achievement gaps, and ensure spending aligns with measurable outcomes.
